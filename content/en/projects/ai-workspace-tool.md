## Background

This direction focuses on a problem often ignored in tool products: reading burden.

## Problem

High functional density does not need to look visually stressed. Many tools become noisy and tiring as features accumulate.

## Approach

A steadier hierarchy, softer but precise emphasis, and fewer decorative moves keep the interface readable even when the state is dense.

## Outcome

The result is a UI approach for content-heavy tools that also feeds back into the rhythm of the personal site.
