It is not difficult to make an interface look impressive once. The harder task is making it feel comfortable after repeated use.

## Why restraint matters

The job of the interface is not to showcase how many tricks the designer knows. It is to shape information into something the user can keep reading.

## Rhythm beats effect

Stable rhythm comes from spacing, hierarchy, and structure rather than decorative motion. Most interface comfort comes from the restraint you barely notice.
